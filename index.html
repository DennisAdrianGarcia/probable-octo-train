Summer Holiday

Baywalk

Well-known seaside promenade lined with palm trees drawing walkers, bicyclists and joggers.

City Coliseum

Now with plants and other vegetables planted, and food, clothing stalls etc. The Puerto Princesa City coliseum is now visited with many people.

Baker's Hill

Baker's Hill is a popular stopover for Puerto Princesa tourists to hoard over baked goods and local snacks (though nothing too special) for their trip back home.

Honda Bay

Honda Bay is one of the most popular tourist attraction in Puerto Princesa well known for its white sand beaches, wonderful landscapes and coral reef, home to abundant marine life.

Mendoza Park

City park with a playground, public art, sculptures and a covered amphitheater for events.

Plaza Cuartel

Outdoor park and site of a formal penal colony, where 150 U.S. soldiers were burned by the Japanese.
